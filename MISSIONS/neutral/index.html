    MISSION CYCLES¶

    001 — LOST CHARM¶

    JANUARY 2022 — VARIOUS LOCATIONS lady costwicks sends out a series of requests for witches to find her missing enchantment trinkets, which have been scattered across delphi city.

    • LADY COSTWICKS MINOR NPC a retired cerberus enchantment specialist. she has a son called charlie, and is the descendant of gammana costwicks, who was known for her spatial enchantments during the great war.

    002 — SAFE HAVEN¶

    JANUARY 2022 — THE SLUMS, DELPHI the new dawn sponsors witches to conduct home repairs for humans in the slums.

    • LANCE minor npc made an appearance in out of scale. a champion of cerberus, apparently investigating the new dawn and finding it wanting.

    003 — IGNITION PT. I¶

    APRIL 2022 — DOWNTOWN DELPHI & TARTARUS a series of scheduled protests around the sybaris-STA deal breaks into a violent riot, fuelled by a rise in anti-witch sentiment.

    • this mission cycle has a special epilogue, that involved dmitri wisbane, farah serizawa, cain delacour, and emma. read about it here.

    004 — DON'T MAKE WAVES¶

    MAY 2022 — WORLD'S END BEACH, DELPHI in preparation for the summer tourist season, the city calls for volunteers to clean up the beach. this year, however, the council asks for volunteering witches not to use their magic, citing concerns over high tensions and rising anti-witch sentiments.

    • LEO VARLEY minor npc made an appearance in i hate sand. an acolyte of cerberus from squadron kappa phi5. accompanied a pair of witches from the vale's cavalry.
    • the epilogues of this mission cycle focus on rumours that characters overhear at the beach.
    • in seaglass, trouble seems to be stirring in the vale, with some blaming the monarchists. interestingly, some things hit closer to home — doesn't pythia have its own rulers too, despite floating right above delphi?
    • in community service again, queenie is one of many witches to receive a prophecy after attending the events of the pegasus afterparty. in it, she seems to witness the death of her covenmate, maddox.
    • in clean up, everybody do your part, some speculate about the augury of the phoenix, wondering about the scarred rupture hanging above the cemetery1.
    • in washed up like seafoam, rumor has it that cerberus witches were seen at bell's2.
    • in stand your ground, some criticise the way in which cerberus frames participation in the upcoming n.e.p.. good in theory, but dubious in execution. what else do you call something that sends its children off to die but a monster?
    • in claw at your heart, it turns out someone is sailing ghost ships from the vale. empty containers — and plenty of space for stowaways.
    • in cub scouts, there appears to be a shift towards maritime transport, due to frequent delays and breakdowns on the cad.
    • in she sells sea shells, some debate perceptions about mayor verna3 — a sellout to delphi? to salem as a whole?
    • in tidying of the heart, the abyssal hunters encounter an accrescent, a messenger from the deep, singing a haunting warning about an invasion.
    • in i hate sand, figures dressed in black and gold4 are escorted by a cerberus witch to retrieve the caelestrix ring that was found by xiomara and vera. the ring is believed to be involved in an on-going investigation.
    • in every day is a festival, a glimpse of an invite-only chat room appears to reveal some conflicting beliefs within members of the new dawn.

    1. the cemetery likely refers to the garden of hades, located in the slums. ↩

    2. "bell's" likely refers to bell's bar, a bar in tartarus. it was also involved in the events of no heroes, a personal quest. ↩

    3. mayor kashvi verna is the mayor of eclipse town. ↩

    4. black and gold are the colours of the vale's cavalry. ↩

    5. leo varley was previously encountered during the events of the pegasus afterparty. ↩
